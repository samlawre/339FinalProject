Comparing Barcelona to London

Barcelona, Spain vs. London, England

Let's compare these two popular study abroad cities side-by-side.

Barcelona Cost of Living

While the cost of living in Barcelona is relatively high compared to the rest of Spain, compared to many European cities it costs significantly less to live and rent in Barcelona. According to Numbeo.com, you would need around 3,309.49£ (3,852.67€) in Barcelona to maintain the same standard of life that you can have with 5,100.00£ in London (assuming you rent in both cities).

London Cost of Living

The cost of living in London is significantly effected by the cities rent prices, as the average rent in London is 52% higher than the average rent in Barcelona. Other cost of living expenses like consumer prices, groceries, and restaurant prices are also between 16% - 35% high in London than Barcelona according to Numbeo.com.

Barcelona Transportation

Transportation in Barcelona and London are very comparable in terms of both cities having strong public transportation systems and being highly walkable cities.
